WHO WE ARE?

The Hidden Gems of BC is a personal tour guide company.


Here at Hidden Gems, we strive to give you a one-of-a-kind B.C. exploration experience. Our tours are suitable for both locals and newcomers who have never visited BC before! We want to show you the natural beauty that BC has to offer and help you create unforgettable memories together.


Our goal at Hidden Gems is to promote tourism in B.C. and show the underrated locations or unexplored spots of British Columbia. We refer to these magical spots as the “Hidden Gem locations
